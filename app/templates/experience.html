    "The MLH Fellowship"

    The MLH Fellowship is a 12-week internship alternative for aspiring technologists. It provides real-world experience by contributing to Open-Source Projects. Our programs pair fun, educational curriculum with practical experience. It's collaborative, remote, and happens under the guidance of expert mentors. The program is divided into tracks that align with different career interests that Fellows have. During Github Universe, Mike Swift, CEO, and Co-Founder of MLH described the fellowship as, “The Future of Tech Internships.”
